Terrafy

This is the survival sandbox game I made for my A-Level coursework. Some notable features include, procedural generation, multithreading and multiplayer support. The player must gather resources, fight randomly spawning enemies and defeat the boss at the end. This game uses the JavaFX library.

Chess

This game allows two players to play each other locally in a game of chess. Pieces can be moved by dragging and dropping or by clicking. The board rotates after each player's turn.
