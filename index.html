Projects

Here you will find some of my personal and school projects with each one having its own dedicated page

Alcohol Tracking App

This is an app I made to track your alcohol consumption and compare with friends. You can create an event with your friends and log all your drinks for the night. I used Flutter for the front-end and Flask/MySQL for the back-end, hosted on AWS.

Terrafy

This is the survival sandbox game I made for my A-Level coursework. Some notable features include, procedural generation, multithreading and multiplayer support. The player must gather resources, fight randomly spawning enemies and defeat the boss at the end.

Chess

This game allows two players to play each other locally in a game of chess. Creating this game was amazing for developing my OOP skills. Pieces can be moved by dragging and dropping or by clicking. The board rotates after each player's turn.
